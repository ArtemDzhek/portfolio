my story
I started to study Front-End in the middle of 2021. Actually, my experience in IT began 11 years ago: I was SEO Copywriter and learned a lot about SEO, so then I started to work as an SEO specialist. In 2020 I relocated to Poland and tried programming. I love it! It's so structured and logical, also, one task has a lot of solutions, so there's always room for creativity. I tried a lot of different jobs in my life:
  • Car Mechanic
  • Turner
  • Furniture maker
  • Plumber
  • Production Worker
  • Nurse in a Pharmacy
  • Photographer
  • Customer Service Representative
  • SEO Copywriter
  • SEO Specialist
I tried different things and liked each of these jobs. Photography is one of my passions, so I was offered a job as a photojournalist and worked at many events for 5 years. Fun fact: I got my Law degree while working as a plumber. As you can see, all these positions require logical or visual skills, or both, like a furniture maker and a turner. So it was a matter of time before I combined them both. I tried myself in Front-End and figured out: this is what I want to do!
